Our Story

Born from a Need for Ethical Recovery

Oak Debt Collectors was founded on the belief that debt recovery doesn't have to be aggressive or undignified. Based in Tlokweng, Gaborone, we serve businesses and individuals across Botswana who need professional, effective, and ethical solutions for recovering outstanding debts.

Our name reflects our values — oak trees are known for their strength and endurance, and our eagle emblem represents precision, sharp vision, and the power to soar above challenges. We bring that same energy to every case we handle.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide professional, ethical, and effective debt recovery and legal consultancy services that protect the financial interests of our clients while maintaining the dignity of all parties involved.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted debt collection and legal consultancy firm in Botswana, recognized for our integrity, results, and commitment to transforming how debt recovery is perceived and practiced.

Our Values

Integrity, professionalism, and respect guide every interaction. We believe doing the right thing and getting results are not mutually exclusive — they reinforce each other.
